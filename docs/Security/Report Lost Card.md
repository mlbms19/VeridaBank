# How do I report a lost or stolen card?

If your card is lost or stolen, *report it immediately* by phone or at the nearest VeridaBank branch. You can also lock access to your card online. 

Unauthorized transactions on your card can occur quickly, and we can help prevent further misuse.

=== "By phone"
    •&nbsp;&nbsp;	Call **1-800-813-1743** immediately.<br>
    •&nbsp;&nbsp;	A VeridaBank representative will lock your card and help issue a replacement.
    ???+ note
        If you receive a call claiming to be from VeridaBank, you can confirm its legitimacy by calling our **Anti-Fraud Hotline (1-800-813-1634)**.

=== "At our branch"
    •&nbsp;&nbsp;	Visit your nearest VeridaBank branch for in-person assistance.

=== "Online (lock your card)"

    1.	Sign in to **VeridaBank Online**.
    2.	Select **Security** from the top navigation.
    3.	Choose **Cards**.
    4.	Select the card you want to lock.
    5.	Click (or tap) **Lock**.
